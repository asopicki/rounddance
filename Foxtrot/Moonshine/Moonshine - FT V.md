# Moonshine
*Song by Katie Melua (Album: Secret Symphony, Track 4, Duration 2:41)*

* **Choreographer**: Alexander Sopicki
* **Dance**: Foxtrot - V (Easy)
* **Sequence**: Intro - A - B - A - B - A1 - Bridge - B - Ending

*Dance starts in left open position (LOP), facing diagonal line and center, lead feet free.*

    <td>

    </td>

      recover R,
      back L;

> Reverse turn; ; Hover; Wing  

<table>
  <tr>
    <th>Measure Cue</th>
    <th>Leader (M)</th>
    <th>Follower (W)</th>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td><em>5-6</em> Reverse turn;;</td>
    <td>
    fwd L heading down LOD comm. to turn LF,-, turning LF sd & slightly bk R, bk L;
    bk R comm to turn turn LF,-, turning LF sd & fwd L preparing to step outside partner, fwd R outside partner to BJO DLW;
    </td>
    <td>
      bk R,-, bringing L to R heel turn LF on R close L, fwd R;
      fwd L comm to turn LF,-, turning LF sd & bk R, bk L under body facing DRC;
    </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td><em>7</em> Hover;</td>
    <td>
      fwd L, -, fwd & side R with slight rise, side and fwd L to SCP;
    </td>
    <td>
      bk R, -, bk & sd L with rise and optional brush with right foot, sd & fwd R to SCP;
    </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td><em>8</em> Wing;</td>
    <td>
      in SCP fwd R, -, draw left to right, touch left to right turning upper part
      of body left face with left side stretch;
    </td>
    <td>
      fwd L beginning to cross in front of M commence turn slightly left, -
      fwd R around M continue to turn slightly left,
      fwd L around M complete slight left face turn to end in sidecar;
    </td>
  </tr>
</table>
